Latin Square : Why drop sum-product with max marginal regret? LDS

Sum-product with max marginal regret is beaten by max product with max marginal regret and sum-product with dom wdeg max marginal.

Sum-product is especially bad for optimality proofs.

vs oracle

  • 1st solution: zero fails for both, score is often higher with oracle (7/10 for 200/250 holes, 10/10 for 300 holes)
  • last solution: equivalent
  • optimality proof: equivalent

Not sure if sum-product should be preferred to max-product with oracle if first solution score is important.

vs max product with max marginal regret

  • 1st solution: found with almost no fail, score is higher with max product
  • last solution: take less fails with max product
  • optimality proof: takes much longuer with sum product

Max product is better than sum product with max marginal regret heuristic.

vs sum product with dom wdeg max marginal

  • 1st solution: found with almost no fail, equivalent quality
  • last solution: all in 100s fails, about 1.5x more fails with max marginal
  • optimality proof: takes much longer (x10-50) with dom wdeg
